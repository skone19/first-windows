\begin{frame}
The derivative of $f(x) = g(x) \cdot h(x)$, with $g(x) =
↪ x^2$ and $h(x) = \sin(x)$ equals
\begin{align*}
f'(x) \uncover<2->{&= g'(x) \cdot h(x) +}
↪ \uncover<3->{g(x) \cdot h'(x)} \\
&\uncover<4->{= 2x \cdot \sin(x) +}
↪ \uncover<5->{x^2\cdot \cos(x).}
\end{align*}
\end{frame}
